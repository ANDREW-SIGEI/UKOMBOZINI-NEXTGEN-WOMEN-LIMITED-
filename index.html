Our Products

Premium Agrochemical Solutions for Superior Crop Performance

Discover our range of high-quality agrochemical products designed to enhance crop nutrition, protect against pests, and maximize agricultural yields.

Super Copper Liquid Xtra

Advanced copper-based fungicide for effective disease control in crops. Provides long-lasting protection and improved plant health.

PHO-SIL

Phosphorus and silica supplement that enhances root development, improves nutrient uptake, and increases crop resilience.

PYR-GOLD

Premium insecticide for comprehensive pest control. Effective against a wide range of insects while being safe for beneficial organisms.

EMPEROR-SILMAX

Silicon-based nutrient enhancer that strengthens plant cell walls, improves drought tolerance, and boosts overall plant vigor.

CHLORO-ZIN

Chlorine and zinc micronutrient supplement that corrects deficiencies, promotes healthy growth, and enhances fruit quality.
