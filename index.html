Welcome to Ukombozini Nextgen Women

Empowering women through financial inclusion

Ukombozini Nextgen Women Limited is dedicated to transforming the lives of women through table banking, financial education, and economic empowerment programs that create sustainable paths to independence.

  • Join our table banking groups

  • Access to micro-loans and top-ups

  • Financial literacy training and business skills development
  • Women-led product development and marketing support
Learn More About Us

Our Services

Empowering Women Through Financial Solutions

We provide comprehensive financial and business solutions designed specifically for women to achieve economic independence and sustainable growth.

Table Banking

Join our community savings groups where members pool resources and provide loans to each other.

Financial Top-Ups

Access additional capital through our top-up program to grow your business or manage emergencies.

Women's Products

Support for developing, marketing, and distributing products created by women entrepreneurs.

Success Stories

What our members are saying

Joining the Ukombozini table banking group changed my life. With my first loan, I expanded my vegetable stand, and now I employ two other women. The financial literacy training helped me understand business management and savings.

Jane Mwangi

Market Vendor, Nairobi

The financial top-up program helped me when my business needed capital during the low season. I received 20,000 Ksh to buy inventory in bulk at better prices. My profits have increased by 40% since joining Ukombozini.

Mary Akinyi

Clothing Retailer, Mombasa

I was struggling to finance my farm until I joined the Agricultural Cooperative group. The weekly meetings provide not just financial support but also a community of women who understand my challenges. My children now attend better schools.

Fatuma Wanjiku
