NEWLEAF AGROCHEMICALS CO LTD is Kenya's leading provider of advanced crop nutrition and pest control solutions.

NEWLEAF AGROCHEMICALS CO LTD is committed to revolutionizing agriculture through innovative agrochemical solutions. Our comprehensive range of products includes advanced fertilizers, pesticides, and crop protection solutions designed to maximize yields and ensure sustainable farming practices. We have successfully served over 10,000 farmers across Kenya, helping them achieve higher productivity and profitability. Through our expert agricultural consulting services, we provide farmers with cutting-edge knowledge, soil testing, and customized treatment plans. Our mission is to empower farmers with the tools they need to thrive in modern agriculture while promoting environmental sustainability.

Dr. James Kiprop

Chief Executive Officer
