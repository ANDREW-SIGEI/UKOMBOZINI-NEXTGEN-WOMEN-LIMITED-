Beyond Copper: A Scientific Breakdown of the Super Copper Xtra Power Formula

What Makes Super Copper Xtra Different? Most copper supplements offer a single-nutrient solution. Super Copper Xtra is a fortified nutritional product, meaning its components are engineered to work in concert, creating a synergistic effect that is greater than the sum of its parts.

The Synergistic Power of 5 Ingredients: A Detailed Breakdown:

  • Copper (Cu - 15%): The cornerstone. As a systematic nutrient, it moves through the plant's vascular system. Its primary role is in enzyme activation for lignin synthesis (building strong cell walls) and chlorophyll production. Crucially, it's a co-factor in the enzymes responsible for pollen viability, directly influencing fruit set and reducing flower abortion.
  • Amino Acids (30%): Think of these as the plant's energy bars. They are the pre-formed building blocks of proteins. By providing them directly, the plant saves the massive amount of energy normally required to synthesize them from scratch. This conserved energy is redirected towards growth, stress recovery, and developing defense compounds.
  • Boron (B - 2.5%): The unsung hero of reproduction. Boron is vital for cell division, pollen tube growth, and seed development. A boron deficiency often manifests as poor fruit set and "blossom drop," where flowers abort before forming fruit. Super Copper Xtra ensures this critical process isn't hindered.
  • Naphthalene Acetic Acid (NAA - 1.25%): A powerful plant growth regulator. It mimics natural auxins, hormones that control root development and fruit retention. It signals the plant to hold onto its developing fruits and flowers, promoting uniform sizing and a more concentrated harvest period.
  • Sulphur (S - 1.5%): Essential for synthesizing cysteine and methionine, two amino acids that are the building blocks of proteins and enzymes. It also plays a key role in the formation of vitamins and co-enzymes, and is a component of the compounds that give plants like onions and garlic their distinctive flavor.

The Combined Benefit: This formula doesn't just feed the plant; it optimizes its entire physiological system. It strengthens structure, supercharges metabolism, ensures successful reproduction, and builds resilience.
