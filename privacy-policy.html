Privacy Policy for Ukombozini Nextgen Women Limited

Last updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

Ukombozini Nextgen Women Limited ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Data Protection Act of Kenya.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (Name, ID number, contact details)
  • Financial information (Bank details, M-PESA number)
  • Group membership details
  • Contribution and loan records
  • Meeting attendance records

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage your table banking group membership
  • Process contributions and loans
  • Communicate about meetings and events
  • Provide financial training and resources
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures comply with Kenyan data protection laws.

5. Your Rights

Under Kenyan data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Group leaders and administrators
  • Financial service providers (for processing payments)
  • Regulatory authorities (when required by law)

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
